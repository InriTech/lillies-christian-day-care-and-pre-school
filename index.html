Welcome to Lillie's Christian Day Care and Pre-School

"Train up a child in the way he should go, and when he is old he will not depart from it." - Proverbs 22:6

Since 2008, Lillie's Christian Day Care and Pre-School has been providing exceptional early childhood education in the heart of Berea, Johannesburg. We offer children a head start through our various stimulative programs focusing on physical, mental, intellectual, social and spiritual development.

Our Mission

To provide a safe, nurturing Christian environment where children can grow and develop to their full potential while learning biblical values that will guide them throughout life.

  • Christian-based curriculum integrating Bible stories and values
  • Safe, secure and nurturing environment
  • Qualified and experienced ECD practitioners
  • Age-appropriate learning activities aligned with NCF
  • Small class sizes for individual attention
  • Nutritious meals included

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions From Parents

We are open Monday to Friday from 6:30 AM to 5:30 PM to accommodate working parents. We are closed on weekends and public holidays.

For babies: Diapers, wipes, 2-3 changes of clothes, bottles/formula if needed, comfort items. For toddlers/preschoolers: Change of clothes, water bottle, sunhat. We provide all meals, bedding, and learning materials.

We use positive discipline techniques focused on teaching rather than punishing. This includes redirection, natural consequences, and teaching problem-solving skills, all within a framework of Christian values and love.

Our facility has controlled access, CCTV cameras, first aid trained staff, regular safety drills, childproofed environments, and strict illness policies. All staff undergo thorough background checks.

Currently we focus exclusively on early childhood development from 3 months to Grade R. We do not offer aftercare for older children, but we're happy to recommend some excellent aftercare programs in our area.
